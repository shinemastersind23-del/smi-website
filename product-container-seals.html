Product Overview

Container rubber seals are the last line of defence against moisture, pests, temperature fluctuation, and cargo contamination. Shine Masters Industries produces heavy-duty container gaskets and door seals that meet the exacting demands of international shipping, domestic logistics, and cold-chain transport.

Our container seals are manufactured from premium EPDM and neoprene rubber compounds, engineered for long-term compression set resistance ‚Äî meaning they maintain their sealing performance even after thousands of door open-close cycles and years of field exposure.

We supply standard ISO container seal profiles as well as custom-designed seals for reefer (refrigerated) containers, tank containers, and specialised cargo units. Our B2B clients include container depots, logistics operators, and shipping line maintenance operations across India.
