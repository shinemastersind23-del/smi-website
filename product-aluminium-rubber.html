Product Overview

Aluminium rubber profiles represent the premium end of the sealing profile market. By bonding extruded aluminium sections with vulcanised rubber sealing elements, SMI creates profiles that combine the structural precision of metal with the dynamic sealing performance of rubber.

These profiles are widely used in automotive glass run channels, where the aluminium carrier provides consistent glass guidance and the rubber lip provides the sealing contact. They are also used in bus body window frames, where weight, aesthetics, and sealing performance all matter.

SMI's aluminium rubber profiles are manufactured with alloy grades selected for the specific application, anodised or powder-coated to customer specification, and bonded with rubber compounds selected for the operating environment.
